## 3. What Kalta is

Kalta is a personal home inventory app for tracking emergency supplies. It lets you:

- Create warehouses and organize items in physical boxes with QR codes.
- Scan barcodes to look up product info from public databases.
- Track expiration dates and receive local reminders.
- Share a warehouse with another user via invitation link.
- Sync data directly with a nearby iPhone via peer-to-peer connection.
- Optionally use AI-assisted product recognition with your own Anthropic API key.

Kalta is **not** a substitute for professional advice about emergency preparedness, food safety, or expiration of medical supplies. You are solely responsible for evaluating the accuracy and suitability of any information displayed.
