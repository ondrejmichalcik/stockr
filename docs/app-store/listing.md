## Description (4000 char max)

```
Kalta is a simple, private home inventory app for tracking emergency supplies in real boxes in your home.

Create a warehouse. Print QR labels for your boxes. Scan barcodes to fill in product info automatically. Add expiration dates. Kalta sorts everything by urgency and reminds you before items expire.

Built for people who actually rotate their stock.

KEY FEATURES

• Boxes with QR labels — Stick a QR label on each physical box. Scan it to jump straight to its contents.
• Barcode scanning — Fills in product name and category from the Open Food Facts database. Custom entries for anything not in the database.
• Expiration tracking — 4-level color coding (expired, soon, OK, good). Items and boxes are sorted by urgency on the main screen.
• Local reminders — Configurable notifications 30 days, 7 days, 1 day before, and on the expiry day. No servers involved — notifications are scheduled on your device.
• Share with family — Invite your partner or family members to a warehouse. Everyone sees updates in real time.
• Offline-first — Everything works without internet. Changes sync when you're back online.
• Peer-to-peer sync — Two iPhones can exchange data directly over Bluetooth and WiFi, no network required.
• AI-assisted product recognition — Optional. Bring your own Anthropic API key and let Claude identify products from a photo. You're in control of cost and privacy.
• Print QR labels — Brother Bluetooth label printer support, or share the QR as an image to print anywhere.

PRIVACY

No ads. No tracking. No analytics. No crash reporting. No third-party SDKs.

Data is stored on your iPhone and synced to an encrypted database hosted in Ireland (EU). Only you and people you explicitly invite to a warehouse can see your inventory.

Apple Sign In is the only account system — no new passwords to manage.

WHAT KALTA IS NOT

Kalta is a personal inventory tool. It is not a substitute for professional advice on emergency preparedness, food storage safety, or medication rotation. Always verify product-specific information from the manufacturer.

SUPPORT

Email: ondrej.michalcik@gmail.com
Website: https://kalta.app
Privacy Policy: https://kalta.app/privacy
Terms of Service: https://kalta.app/terms
```

## Screenshots (to be captured)

**Required sizes:**

- **6.7" Display** — iPhone 15 Pro Max, iPhone 16 Pro Max (1290 × 2796 px portrait, 2796 × 1290 landscape)
- **6.1" Display** — iPhone 15, iPhone 16 (1179 × 2556 portrait, 2556 × 1179 landscape)

**Recommended number:** 4–6 screenshots per size. More is fine (Apple allows up to 10).

**Screens to capture (order matters — first 3 show in search results):**

1. **Dashboard** — Warehouses list with at least 2 warehouses, some with urgent items showing in the badge or status indicator. Caption overlay suggestion: *"Organize emergency supplies in real boxes."*
2. **Box detail** — A box's contents list with items of varying expiry colors. Caption: *"Expiration tracked by urgency."*
3. **Barcode scanner** — Scanner UI active with a product just scanned. Caption: *"Scan to add in seconds."*
4. **QR label preview / print** — Generated QR label for a box. Caption: *"Print QR labels for every box."*
5. **Invitation / sharing** — Settings tab showing members + invite button. Caption: *"Share with your family."*
6. *(Optional)* **AI feature** — AI suggestion sheet after photo scan. Caption: *"Optional AI help, your own key."*

**Guidelines:**

- Use real-looking data (not "Lorem ipsum"). Suggest: Canned beans, Rice, First aid kit, Batteries, Water.
- Light mode for screenshots (better App Store rendering — Apple's own apps do this).
- Caption overlays optional but increase conversion; can generate via tools like Fastlane Screengrab or by hand in Figma.
