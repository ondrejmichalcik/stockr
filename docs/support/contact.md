# Contact

Need help with Kalta, or want to report a problem?

Please include:

- A short description of what you were trying to do.
- What happened instead.
- Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About).
- Your Kalta app version (Settings → bottom of the screen).
- Screenshot if relevant.

## What to email about

- Bug reports.
- Questions about sharing, syncing, or any app feature.
- Data export requests (GDPR right to portability).
- Account deletion requests (GDPR right to erasure).
- Privacy-related questions or requests.
- Feature suggestions.

## What NOT to email about

- **App purchase refunds** — Apple handles those. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com.
- **Apple ID / iCloud issues** — contact Apple Support.
- **Anthropic API key or billing issues** — contact Anthropic support at https://support.anthropic.com.

## Response time

We're a one-person operation. We aim to reply within **3–5 business days**.
